Quiet Mornings, Better Decisions

Feb 26, 2026 · 2 min read

When my morning starts with inputs, I spend the day reacting. When it starts with silence, I decide.

Even 20 minutes of quiet planning changes everything: clearer priorities, less panic, better follow-through.

I don’t need a perfect routine. I need a repeatable one that protects my first decisions.
